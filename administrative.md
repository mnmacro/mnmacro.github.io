# Administrative Information

<p>Please don't leave personal belongings unattended. Items left behind may not be present when you return.</p>

<p><strong>Restrooms:</strong> Restrooms are located across from the elevators on the first floor of Hanson Hall.</p>

<p><strong>Minneapolis–St. Paul Light Rail:</strong> The Light Rail offers easy access to and from the airport, hotel, and the University of Minnesota. The standard adult fare is $2.00. More information is available at <a href="https://www.metrotransit.org/fares">www.metrotransit.org/fares</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Uber and Lyft:</strong> Both rideshare services are widely available throughout the Twin Cities.</p>

<p><strong>Please wear your nametag!</strong> Your nametag allows you to be identified by staff as a participant of the conference. This is particularly important at the dinner and for the staff who will be giving out lunches.</p>

## Wi-Fi access — connecting to eduroam

<ol>
  <li>On your device, navigate to the area where you can view all Wi-Fi networks that are in range.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>eduroam</strong> from the list of available networks.</li>
  <li>If your home institution participates in eduroam, log in with your home institution credentials.</li>
</ol>

## Wi-Fi access — connecting to UofM-Guest

<ol>
  <li>On your device, navigate to the area where you can view all Wi-Fi networks that are in range.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>UofM-Guest</strong> from the list of available Wi-Fi networks.</li>
  <li>A web browser will automatically open to a network authentication page. This page explains the Terms of Use you agree to by using the guest Wi-Fi.</li>
</ol>

<p>Is your device asking you for additional information? Use the following settings:</p>
