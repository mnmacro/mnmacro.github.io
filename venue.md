# Venue

<p>The conference will take place on the {{ site.conference.venue_html }}.</p>

<p>Restrooms are located across from the elevators on the first floor of Hanson Hall.</p>

<h2>Wednesday dinner</h2>

<p>On Wednesday, July 29, dinner will be held at <strong>6:00 PM</strong> at the <a href="https://wam.umn.edu/">Weisman Art Museum</a> (333 East River Parkway, on the East Bank of the University of Minnesota's Twin Cities campus). The Weisman is about a 15-minute walk from Hanson Hall across the Washington Avenue Bridge.</p>

<h2>Getting there</h2>

<p>The Metro Transit light rail offers easy access between the Minneapolis–St. Paul airport, downtown hotels, and the University of Minnesota. The standard adult fare is $2.00. More information is available at <a href="https://www.metrotransit.org/fares">www.metrotransit.org/fares</a>.</p>

<p>Uber and Lyft are also widely available throughout the Twin Cities.</p>
