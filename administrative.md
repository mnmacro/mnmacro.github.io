# Logistics

## At the conference

<p><strong>Meals:</strong> Breakfast and lunch will be served each day of the conference. A conference dinner will be held on Wednesday evening, and a reception on Thursday evening. Beyond the conference events, there are many good restaurants throughout the Minneapolis area.</p>

<p><strong>Personal belongings:</strong> Please don't leave personal belongings unattended. Items left behind may not be present when you return.</p>

<p><strong>Restrooms:</strong> Restrooms are located across from the elevators on the first floor of Hanson Hall.</p>

<p><strong>Please wear your nametag!</strong> Your nametag allows you to be identified by staff as a participant of the conference. This is particularly important at the dinner and for the staff who will be giving out lunches.</p>

## Getting around

<p><strong>Uber and Lyft:</strong> Both rideshare services are widely available throughout the Twin Cities.</p>

<p><strong>Minneapolis–St. Paul Light Rail:</strong> The Light Rail offers easy access to and from the airport, hotel, and the University of Minnesota. The standard adult fare is $2.00. More information is available at <a href="https://www.metrotransit.org/fares">www.metrotransit.org/fares</a>.</p>

## Wi-Fi access

<p>The University of Minnesota provides <strong>eduroam</strong>. If your home institution participates, log in with your home institution credentials.</p>

<p>A <strong>UofM-Guest</strong> network is also available — see <a href="https://it.umn.edu/services-technologies/how-tos/wifi-guest-access">it.umn.edu/services-technologies/how-tos/wifi-guest-access</a> for details.</p>
