<p>Sessions are held on the first floor of Hanson Hall (1925 4th Street S, Minneapolis), on the west bank of the University of Minnesota's Twin Cities campus. Restrooms are across from the elevators.</p>

<p>The <a href="https://wam.umn.edu/">Weisman Art Museum</a> (333 E River Road, Minneapolis) is on the east bank, about a 15-minute walk from Hanson Hall across the Washington Avenue Bridge.</p>

<h2>Getting there</h2>

<p>Hanson Hall is a short walk (about 10 minutes) from the <a href="/hotel/">conference hotel</a>.</p>

<p>Uber and Lyft are also widely available throughout the Twin Cities.</p>
